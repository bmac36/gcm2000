Benefits of Good Communication

Communication is a key skill to master for arriving and thriving in your career. The purpose of communication is to share information effectively. We communicate in many different ways—through speaking, writing, signaling—both verbally and non-verbally. When we share information well, we develop and sustain relationships with those around us.

Communicating to Arrive

On the job hunt, having strong communication skills will help you to:

  • Refine your ability to conduct self-assessments, research, and network
  • Define your personal brand, develop your job search tools, and interview effectively

Communicating to Thrive

On the job, good communication skills will help you to:

  • Communicate in teams
  • Share information with management
  • Express your career development plan
  • Complete daily work

Each of these activities requires the transfer of information, so it only makes sense that mastering communication skills will help you to perform them better.

Regardless of what you are communicating, you need to be accurate, clear, and have a purpose. Strong communication skills take a lifetime to develop, and there is always room for improvement.
