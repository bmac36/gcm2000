Classroom Activities

Communication Styles Discussion

Time Required: 45 minutes

This week’s face-to-face class will begin with a discussion of communication styles. Students can complete the discussion exercises in their groups and debrief as a large group once completed.

PowerPoint Slide Group Activity

Time Required: 60 minutes

  1. Working in your groups, design two PowerPoint slides using the tips outlined in the Effective Team Presentations ppt and the CNIB guidelines, covering one of the concepts in our course.
  2. Provide a short explanation for why you designed your slides the way you did and give us a sense of the verbal script that would accompany the slide. Identify which section of the course you were addressing.
  3. Practice your delivery of this brief presentation and be prepared to present in the second part of today’s class time.

Social Media Discussion/Debate

Time Required: 45 minutes

Questions:

  • What are advantages and shortcomings of electronic communication?
  • How often do I consider the medium for my own message delivery?
  • What are the challenges and opportunities of social networking technology for employees and employers?

Beyond the Classroom

Blog Post for Week 11

For your weekly blog, reflect upon the following questions related to culture and communication:

  1. Workplaces often possess different and unusual customs depending on the industry and location. Consider your industry in Canada. What aspects of workplace culture do you find challenging in this industry? How do you intend to deal with this challenge?
  2. Think of a time when you experienced a breakdown in communication. Why was that? What could you do differently to improve communications?
  3. How do you see yourself in terms of understanding intercultural communication issues? Would you consider yourself to be high in intercultural intelligence skills? Why?
