Communicating with Social Media

Social Media Prominence

In the past few years, prominent psychologists studying communication theory have found that people are actually becoming more social and more interactive with others, but the style of that communication has changed so that weâ€™re not meeting face-to-face as often as we used to do. Social media is an increasingly common forum for the transmission of messages from a communicator to an audience. The ease of use and ability to share different media with many individuals or groups at once has made this forum a preferred mode of communication.

Using the Right Platform

Choosing which platform to use in any given moment depends on what you are trying to communicate. You may wish to reinforce your personal brand, connect with employers, promote a product, share life updates, or keep in touch with family. Each of these actions may be best suited to a different platform.
