Create a Personal Brand Statement

You’ve already completed the "Discover" step in personal branding through your self-assessment activities in Week 2. The next step is crafting your messaging and defining key words that describe you best. A personal brand statement combines your self-image, others’ perception of you, and what you want to become. It is your unique promise of value to potential employers and one of the most important pieces of your personal brand. Getting this statement right before speaking to employers is critical. It will help you to guide your communications and position your brand consistently.

Review Jenn’s personal brand statement to get some ideas about how to craft your own.

As a sales specialist and amateur marathon runner, I enjoy the challenge of pushing myself to the limits. I’m constantly training my body and mind to adapt to different conditions, whether it’s on the road or in the conference room. I like to go the distance, both crossing the finish line and closing the deal.

Jenn

Reflective Questions

  1. What is unique and appealing about Jenn?
  2. What type of employee do you think Jenn would be?
  3. What type of worker does Jenn aspire to be?
