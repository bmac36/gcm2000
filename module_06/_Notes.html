Discussion Group

Please answer the following questions for your initial post:

Think of brands that have impacted you:

  1. What was the brand
  2. What was the situation that made you consider/interact with the brand?
  3. How was the experience (negative or positive)?
  4. What did the experience cause you to do about that brand?
  5. What words are associated with the brand?
  6. What feelings did you have?
