Compelling

The key thing that branding is really about is differentiating yourself. It’s about not being afraid to stand apart, highlight that and to make it a powerful thing ... and memorable. Then connect your brand to the interests of potential employers. Consider what they want and how to angle yourself to be the most attractive to them. That will help make you successful.

Jenn

Authentic

Success is about becoming who you were meant to be. The trick to effective self-branding is developing a strategy that helps you to achieve your professional and personal goals but also stays true to you. People can tell pretty quickly when you are inauthentic. Don’t oversell your abilities! A good brand authentically represents who you are now, while conveying who you will be as a future employee, co-worker, and leader.

Anwar

Consistent

You want your brand to create the same positive experience for potential employers regardless of setting. Delivering consistent images of professionalism will help potential employers to trust you and “buy your brand” whether you meet them networking, at an informational interview, through your job application materials, social media, or at an interview.

Charizar
