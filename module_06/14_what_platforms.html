What Platforms are You Using?

If you’re not the most tech-savvy person, you might feel overwhelmed with the vast number of social media platforms out there these days. For the purpose of establishing a visible personal brand, you might want to stick to the “Big Four ”: LinkedIn, Twitter, Google+, and Facebook.

Guidelines for Using Social Media

It’s better to use one to two social media accounts regularly and professionally, than try to handle four or more accounts poorly or irregularly. If going for volume, consider a dashboard to handle cross-platform communications . If you are more comfortable, consider branching out to Pinterest, Instagram, Reddit, etc.
