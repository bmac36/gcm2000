LinkedIn

LinkedIn has become the number one site for networking. In 2010, 86% of companies hiring used it to find and attract candidates. If you are not on it, here’s a list of 10 reasons why you should be . As a student or new graduate it is a great place to display your skills, interests, education, experience, and even work or project examples.
