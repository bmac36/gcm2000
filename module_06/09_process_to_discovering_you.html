The Process to Discovering You

The personal branding process allows you to discover who you are, create a consistent image that speaks to your target audience (potential employers), and makes you consistently visible. It allows you to put together a coherent package that positions you as a great product with hiring managers.

Click each step in the process to learn more.

Discover

Discover

  • What you want to do in life
  • Your strengths, passions and goals
  • How others perceive you
  • What appeals to your target audience

Create

Create

  • Your professional image
  • Messaging that is compelling, authentic and consistent
  • Collateral: business card, resume, LinkedIn profile etc.

Communicate

Communicate

  • Make yourself visible online and in-person
  • Actively network
  • Join relevant online communities (e.g. through social media)
