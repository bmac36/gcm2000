6

Audit Yourself Online

Before you take the next steps towards using social media to land a job, take a few moments to critique yourself. What does your digital brand say about your personal brand? Does your digital brand agree with your personal brand statement? What would an employer think if they googled you? Make sure that who you are online is the person they will want to hire. If you look at your social media profiles and realize that they do not project the right message, then it’s time for some damage control.

Audit Yourself Online

1.

1. Google yourself , what exactly comes up?

You might be surprised to see things that you thought were long gone are still floating around cyberspace. Try to use simple personal Search Engine Optimization techniques to bring your personal brand to the top of search results.

2.

2. Check your social media influence through tools like Klout.com ,
PeerIndex , or Kred .

3.

3. Check your privacy settings .

If there are things you don’t want to be seen, make sure they aren’t accessible to the public, or better yet, just delete them.

A word of caution: don’t delete “who” you are. It is important that employers can still get to know you, so don’t feel the need to hide your interests and passions. Just think, by clearing out the more negative stuff it helps make more room for you to display what makes you, you.
