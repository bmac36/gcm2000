Criteria for Selecting a Job Posting

To help you select a job posting that is well-suited to you, consider the following.

1.

1. Identify the qualifications, skills, abilities, company profile, and additional details listed in the posting.

2.

2. Consider your strengths, interests, objectives, and values. Review your self-assessments, accomplishment statements, 30-second pitch, and personal branding statement as a start.

Is this job a good fit for your career goals right now? Is the company a good fit for your career goals right now?

3.

3. Consider your education and experience.

Did your studies contribute to learning skills that you will use on this job? For your work, did you perform any of these duties? Do you possess the required technical and transferable skills? Do you possess the stated qualifications?
