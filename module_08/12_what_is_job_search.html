What is a Job Search Strategy?

Up until this point we have focused on various job search tips and techniques for you to deploy in your own job search. Now let’s look at how to create a comprehensive job search strategy. Mei Lu, the CEO of Jobfully.com defines a Job Search Strategy as:

The art and science of planning and purposefully arranging a job seeker’s resources for their most efficient and effective use toward getting the best job possible at a given time.

Mei Lu, CEO of Jobfully.com

In other words, a job search strategy is putting together the tips and techniques into a comprehensive system. Having an effective job search strategy requires using multiple techniques to uncover opportunities and connect with employers. The most effective job seekers explore a wide variety of avenues to find work; and they also combine many different activities to maximize their chances.

When I was searching for my first professional job, I spent my time trying four different activities: I set up social visits with my extended family and friends so that they knew I was looking for work and obtained word of mouth referrals. I also spent time looking up businesses that I found doing employer research and reviewing their job posting boards for traditional opportunities, and seeking out alumni to meet up for an informational interview – “Coffee and a Chat”, as I called it.

Anwar
