Personal Employment Strategy Portfolio Assignment

This assignment is designed to help students collate their job search, networking, and social media materials into a single strategy portfolio.

This portfolio shall include:

  • Identification of key occupation, employers (from Week 3 Scavenger Hunts)
  • Research and analysis of industry/sector trends and related labour market information (from Week 4 Worksheet)
  • Networking and Informational Interviews Summary (from Week 5 Worksheet)
  • Professional Branding Statement (from Week 6 Worksheet)
  • "30-Second Pitch" (from Week 6 Worksheet)
  • Social Media Inventory (from Week 6 Worksheet)

Use the Personal Employment Strategy Portfolio worksheet to collate the above information.
