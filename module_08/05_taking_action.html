Job Search Strategy

8

Taking A.C.T.I.O.N.

Whatever your goals happen to be, achieving the goals will require A.C.T.I.O.N.
(from No Canadian Experience, Eh? Pg. 21)

Click each letter to find out what the acronym means.

  • A
  • C
  • T
  • I
  • O
  • N

Abandon

ABANDON negative attitudes and fears. Embrace all that is positive and uplifting.

Commit

COMMIT to your goals. Total commitment is necessary for success.

Timeframe

Set a TIMEFRAME within which to accomplish each goal.

Invest

INVEST (time and or money) into yourself and the future. Do whatever it takes to get to where you want to go.

Outcome

The OUTCOME rests with out. You can get help and guidance from others, but only you can make it happen.

Nots

Eliminate the NOTS that have prevented you from moving forward. Visualize the "new you". Act as if you have already obtained your goal.

References:
Wright, D. (2011). No Canadian Experience, Eh? A career success guide for new immigrants. Brampton, ON: WCS, 21.
