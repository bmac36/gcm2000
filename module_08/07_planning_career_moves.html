Planning Your Career Moves

Planning to ascend the career ladder means planning to make upward moves. You will qualify for these moves by developing skills, experience, and qualifications. Pay attention to what is required of the next step in the career ladder and develop a plan to climb towards this position.

Learning to climb the career ladder means that you will need to learn how to find balance with your timing and people skills; don’t wait too long, and be proactive, but don’t have unreasonable expectations. Make your desire and your vision clear to your supervisor, but be tactful. Don’t be pushy, but don’t be too meek or quiet.

Ways to Take Initiative

Even without an official promotion or job change, you can take the following steps to develop skills and connections:

  • Identify and ask for development opportunities.
  • Volunteer in your community.
  • Join meetup groups and hobby clubs.
