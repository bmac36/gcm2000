Job Search Strategy

Career Goals Planning

Use the Career Goals Planning Worksheet to complete the following three sections of information.

How to


1.

1. Decide What You Want to Do: Setting Career Goal

  • Write down a SMART goal.
  • Indicate the benefits and costs of achieving this goal.

2.

2. Turn It Into Smaller Goals: Specific Objectives to Achieve Goal

  • Set three specific objectives you will set that will help you to reach your goal.

3.

3. Work Towards It: Plan to Meet Objectives

  • For each of the three objectives that you set, indicate three steps you will take to meet the objective.
  • Set a target date for meeting your objective.

Columns are provided for you to indicate once you have completed the objective (at a future date).
