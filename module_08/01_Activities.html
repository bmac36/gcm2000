Week 8 Activities

Pre-Class (Online)
3 hours

Student

  • Read through all topics and complete activities
  • Complete the Career Goals Planning Worksheet
  • Find a job posting and complete the Job Posting Worksheet
  • Complete the Job Strategies Worksheet

Instructor

  • Send out weekly email
  • Prepare for class

In-Class
3 hours

Student

  • Career Goals Discussion
  • Job Search Strategies Game
  • Navigating Job Search Obstacles Group Planning
  • Attitudes Brainstorming Session
  • Mindfulness Exercise

Instructor

  • Facilitate class discussions, activities, and discussions

Post-Class
9 Hours

Student

  • Post answers to Group Discussion questions
  • Reply to each team's post
  • Complete blog entry
  • Upload completed Job Strategies Worksheet to blog

Instructor

  • Review discussion forum and blogs and provide feedback

Note that weekly activities are subject to change. Refer to the Instructional Plan or speak to your Instructor for current information.
