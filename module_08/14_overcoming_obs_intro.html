Overcoming Obstacles: Introduction

Having a successful job search strategy takes a lot of work. You need to spend time and effort to perfect your job search techniques. You need to devote time each week to applying each technique at events, online, and in meetings. You need to plan, track, and adjust your strategy to respond to changing conditions. Many job seekers encounter various obstacles during a job search that can make this all the more difficult. Familiarize yourself with the common obstacles faced by job seekers everywhere to adopt a winning approach.

Jenn

An obstacle is simply anything that makes the task of obtaining, enhancing or retaining a job more difficult.

Some obstacles are barriers that may seriously hinder the job search.

Other obstacles are less serious and more "superficial".

Some obstacles are external — meaning we cannot control the condition, which exists outside of ourselves.

Other obstacles are internal — meaning these are conditions that exist within ourselves, which we can respond to or resolve.
