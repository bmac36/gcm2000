Instructions for Week 8 Blog Assignment

For your blog this week:

1.

1. Use the Job Search Strategy Worksheet to develop a useful job search plan using at least three job search strategies, considering industry dynamics, and including a discussion of the research you must undertake to secure employment.

2.

2. Take some time to consider your requirements or targets for a future job such as location, job industry opportunities, and your personal timeline for landing a position as well as being realistic about how much time you can actually dedicate to your search.

3.

3. Some examples of job search strategies include making networking connections through LinkedIn and Indeed.ca, joining Conestoga College Alumni Groups, setting up informational meetings with new contacts, using industry-specific job search sites, attending career fairs or company information sessions on-campus, etc.

4.

4. After posting your completed Career Goals Planning and Job Strategies Worksheet in your blog, discuss what challenges you expect to face in following your job search plan. Then discuss how you plan to overcome these challenges.
