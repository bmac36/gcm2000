How Safe Is Your Job?

To kick off our study of market trends, listen to the Freakonomics podcast How Safe Is Your Job? (2015), where you will learn about the rise and fall of the piano industry and how it relates to economic patterns today.

This podcast touches upon the idea of trends and Labour Market Information. Understanding trends is a critical component of planning your success in specific industries or sectors.

Reflective Questions

  1. Imagine yourself in 1914: Would it be better to be in the piano industry or the radio industry? How about 1919 or 1933? How does this change over time?
  2. What new technologies challenged the prevalence of pianos and why? How does this relate to today?
  3. What industries are being challenged today? Is there a way to predict whether we are in an industry that is challenged by new technology?
