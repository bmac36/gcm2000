Environmental Trends

Global climate change is one of the main environmental trends of our time. It means potentially dramatic changes to social, economic, and political life for all residents of earth and potentially disastrous consequences for the labour market .

Here are some of the ways climate change continues to impact the labour market:

  • An increased focus on commitment to sustainability .
  • Potential contractions for carbon-emitting industries.
  • Slower growth for polluting or environmentally unsustainable employers.
  • Growing opportunities in sustainability and environmental fields, alternative energy and high tech sectors .
