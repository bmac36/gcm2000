Reflective Activity: Lang’s Tannery

To get you thinking about the value of history, research the Lang Tannery Building in Kitchener. It used to house the Lang Tanning Company and has since been repurposed into a world-class technical facility.



Read the article Old tannery building gets new life and answer the questions below in your own notes for in-class discussion. (Also feel free to do additional research on the Internet and using the Conestoga Library Resource Centre to answer the questions below.)

Reflective Questions

  1. When was the Lang Tanning Company founded? What products did it manufacture?
  2. Why did the business close? What did that say about the changing economy at the time?
  3. How is it being used today?
  4. What type of employees occupy the building now?
  5. How do their skills compare to the skills of the tannery workers? What does this reveal about the economy today? What does it tell you about the future?
