Passport Instructions

Every three weeks you will complete one of the 15 passport activites and reported on it using this tool. For each activity you must answer the following questions:

  1. What did you do? Describe the activity. (Answer the quesitons Who? What? Where? When?)
  2. Describe an essential employability skill that you have developed as a result of attending this event.
  3. How does this help you as a job seeker? (I met a new contact. I added something to my résumé;. I learned a new skill that will make me a better employee.)
  4. Write this as an accomplishment statement on your résumé using the STAR method.
