Overcoming Interview Anxiety

Click each flip card to learn more about interview nerves and what you can do to overcome them.

Pre-interview anxiety is common. Why?

Stress often results from a fear of the unknown. The stakes are high and someone else is in control. You don’t know what the interviewer is going to ask or how he is going to behave. This can lead to a feeling of helplessness and increased nervousness.

what can you do to build your confidence?

Preparation can help you to overcome interview nerves. Your job is to minimize the "unknown" by familiarizing yourself with the company and making educated guesses about what will happen during the interview. This can increase your confidence and even help you to look forward to interviewing.
