What is a Job Interview?

A job interview is the most common selection method for companies looking to fill job openings. When a job opening occurs, the employer will post the opening and invite applications; the employer will review the applications and rank each applicant from most suitable to least suitable. Recruiters/employers usually invite some of the most suitable job candidates to attend an interview. Interviews are often held for multiple candidates. The top candidate from the interview will be offered the job.

Your networking and research plus your résumé and cover letter will get you an interview. The interview is what will set you apart from the competition and help you secure the job.

What does a job interview look like?

An interview can be many things:

  • Casual conversation
  • Formal assessment
  • A "Question & Answer" or information session

It can also look like all these things at once for both interviewer and interviewee — as an applicant, you are often assessing, questioning and gathering information about the employer at the same time as they are assessing, questioning and gathering information about you.
