Interviews and Job Offers

In-Class Interview Preparation

Be prepared to participate in mock or practice interviews in your face-to-face class this week.


1.

1. Gather your job ad, targeted cover letter, and targeted résumé to bring to class.

2.

2. Select three interview questions from the Typical Interview Questions and Responses document that you could potentially use as an interviewer, since you will be performing both roles in class.

3.

3. Watch this short video segment on the importance of rehearsing for a job interview from the Films on Demand database.
