Interviews and Job Offers

9

Introduction

The journey towards arriving in a career position requires multiple steps. So far, we have focused on some background preparation and job search techniques that can help you to identify opportunities and respond effectively to them. Your networking and research plus your résumé and cover letter will get you an interview. The interview is what will set you apart from the competition and help you secure the job. This week, we will look at the final steps towards obtaining a career position: interviewing and receiving a job offer.

Learning Outcomes

  • (7.1) Produce a strategy to identify, prepare and succeed in a job interview.
  • (7.2) Identify stages of the interview process (pre-, during, post-), the types of interviews, and the employer’s objectives during an interview.
  • (7.3) Refine communication skills needed for interviews.
  • (7.4) Respond appropriately to different types of interview questions, including standard and behaviour-based questions.
