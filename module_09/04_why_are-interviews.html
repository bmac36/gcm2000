Interviews and Job Offers

9

Why are Interviews Necessary?

Employers rarely hire an employee without first interviewing them.

Consider what the interview gives a company:

  • A better sense of your technical skills.
    These are important for measuring your ability to perform the job duties effectively
  • A better sense of your interpersonal skills and characteristics.
    These are important for deciding whether you will be a good fit with the company’s workplace culture.
  • The chance to learn more about you from your body language.
    More than 60% of what you say has nothing to do with your words – it comes from how you say it and what your body language says!
  • A measure of your interest in and enthusiasm for the role and company.
    Both of those attitudes are very important to an employer, because they are connected to your performance on the job.

Important Note:
An interview is also a good opportunity for you to assess the company, and decide whether or not you want to work for them.
