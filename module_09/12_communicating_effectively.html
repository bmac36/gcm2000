Communicating Effectively in Interviews

Carefully choosing your spoken words iscritical during a job interview. But written communication and body language, or nonverbal communication, are very important too.

Be aware of the body language that you use in your interview. This is likely to change depending on your level of comfort, so self-monitor to ensure you are being consistent. Similarly, be aware of your vocal communicationâ€”tone, volume, pace as well as your word choice. Consider reviewing the content on your Personal Brand regarding professionalism and self-awareness to prepare for communicating during your interview.

Remember the key self-awareness questions:

  • What do I look like?
  • What do I sound like?
  • What do I say?
  • How well do I listen?
