9

Types of Job Interviews

Job interviews can take many different forms depending on the type of job, preferences of the employer, and industry standards or norms. The types of job interviews you are likely to encounter include the following.

Click the information signs to review descriptions of each interview type.

Common Types of Job Interviews

One-on-One

Tip: Demonstrate how your skill set and experience relate to the job.

Panel

Tip: Share your attention with all interviewers through eye contact.

Follow-up

Tip: Research who you will speak to and devise questions that show your interest.

Less Common Types of Job Interviews

Telephone

Tip: Keep your resume by the phone. It will help you to answer questions better.

Technical Testing

Tip: Sleep well the night before and stay calm. The employer just wants to see how you will act "on the job".

Group

Tip: Use good body language, interpersonal and teamwork skills to stand out from the crowd.
