Billy Bishop

  • Credited with 72 victories
  • Recipient of Victoria Cross
  • Billy Bishop enjoyed going on solo patrols into German-controlled areas and claimed to take out numerous German planes.

Raymond Collishaw

  • Credited with 60 victories
  • Collishaw flew long-range bombing missions and was the first pilot to claim six victories in one day.

Donald MacLaren

  • Credited with 54 victories
  • After the war, he helped form the Royal Canadian Air Force before retiring to civil aviation in the inter-war years.

William Barker

  • Credited with 50 victories
  • Recipient of Victoria Cross
  • Barker is notable for achieving the most victories with the same aircraft, a Sopwith Camel (serial number B6313).
