eLearnReady Assessment Survey

Complete the eLearnReady Assessment to analyze your degree of readiness to participate in this online course and receive tips that can help you to be successful.

Review the two steps in the process below:

Step 1: Complete the free eLearnReady assessment online survey. It is smartphone friendly and contains 43 items.

Note: If your instructor sends you an alternate online survey link for your specific class, use this link instead. This allows your instructor to see aggregate results for your class.

This survey is designed to analyze your degree of readingess to participate in online courses. It will help you to assess yourself on nine measurable factors:

  1. Self-Motivation
  2. Self-Management
  3. Feedback
  4. Interaction
  5. Reading: Visual Text
  6. Reading: Visual Graphics
  7. Listening
  8. Technology
  9. Course Management System

Step 2: After you complete the eLearnReady assessment, you will receive a report that analyzes your readiness levels for the nine critical factors and provides study tips based on your score.

You can use this custom report to better prepare yourself for online learning. A link is also provided that lets you share your report with your instructor.
