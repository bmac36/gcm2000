Week 1 Facilitator's Notes

Purpose

  • Introduce course, assignments and expectations
  • Introduce course content and garner interest
  • Create excitement about the personal journey to enhance employability to “arrive and thrive” at a career job in an applicable field
  • Highlight the importance of group work, and participation
  • Emphasize the importance of team work
  • Review post-class assignments

Instructional Strategies

Required Student Prep

  • Week 1 topics and activities
  • Complete online Team Style Quiz

Required Facilitator Prep

  • None

Classroom Activities

Click to reveal an explanation for each activity.

Welcome Ice Breaker

Time Required: 10 minutes

Each student will work with a partner to share answers to the following questions. Each person will introduce their partner to the class.

  • Name
  • Hometown (and country)
  • Something unique about you
  • What you would like to achieve in this course

Individual Team Worksheet

Time Required: 30 minutes

After randomly placing students in groups of 3-5 students, have each team member review the findings of their quiz and individual worksheet.

Beyond the Classroom

Discussion Group

Once they have completed the team contract, students should discuss their answers to this week’s in-class discussion in their teams. After class, students will post their answers in in their Group Discussion Forum. They must also post a reply to each team member’s postings to promote robust discussion. Weekly postings should be between 250-400 words.

Discussion Questions

  1. According to the Team Player Quiz, what kind of a team member are you? What did you learn about yourself?
  2. Describe your previous experiences working in a group or team. What was that like? What role(s) did you play? How successful was the outcome? Looking back, describe what you would do differently.
  3. Have you ever completed a team contract before? How do you see the contract you completed with your team this week contributing to the outcome of your teamwork in this course? Explain how you plan to help your group be successful in its tasks this term.
  4. After reviewing the course outline and assignments, what do you hope to learn this semester? What are you most excited about experiencing? What concerns, if any, do you have?

Blog Post for Week 1

Don’t forget to set up your Self-Awareness and Employability Blog this week and make your first posting, which should be a welcome message. Video tutorials on how to use ePortfolio are included in the Introduction section. Before posting anything in either your Group Discussion Forum or in your blog, be sure to read the article “Rules to Post By”.

Finish by asking if anyone has any further questions. Explain the importance of preparation before the next class.
