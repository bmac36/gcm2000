Productive Work Teams

Teamwork focuses on building relationships among team members with the aim of achieving common objectives and finding solutions to shared problems. Teams combine the talents of many individuals and hold team members accountable to one another. They use a variety of communication processes to share information, knowledge, and experience. Formal processes such as record keeping, facilitation, and scheduled meetings are used to meet team objectives.
