Your Self-Awareness and Employability Blog

To help you establish career goals and objectives and prepare to adjust to the realities of the Canadian labour market, you will keep a Self-Awareness and Employability Blog throughout this course.

Quick Facts

Click each card below to learn more about this important course component.

Posting Requirements

You will create your blog during the first week of the course and will be responsible for making weekly entries throughout the term. Each post must include a minimum of 500 words. Post a welcome message to your blog this week.

Setup

You will set up your blog in eConestoga using the ePortfolio tool. Your classmates and instructor will need to be given access to your blog.

Ultimate Goal

Through the process of blogging you will establish your career goals and objectives and generate your personal employment strategy for life after college.

Tracking Progress

You will use this blog to document your career preparation and learning as you make your way through this course. Each week you will be given some specific questions to answer. This will help you to keep track of your learning, insights, accomplishments, and challenges throughout the term.

Reflective Practice

You will use your blog to practice reflective thinking and writing on topics that are determined by the instructor each week and related to the content of the unit.
