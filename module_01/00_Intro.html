Introduction

Many pathways to finding a profession exist, but not all of them lead to success. How will you chart a course to the job that best matches your skills, personality, and interests? This week you will learn how this course will help you to find and keep a job in your career field. You will be challenged to see your career development as a journey and to apply your best efforts and truest self at all times. You will learn about the importance of maintaining a good digital brand, why you should commit to the practice of self-reflection through blogging, and how to effectively work with team members.

Learning Outcomes

  • (4) Apply professional career management tools, techniques, and strategies, using current technologies, to position oneself as an ideal candidate with targeted employers.
  • (6) Develop workplace communication, teamwork, and self-management skills that reflect best practice, cultural awareness, and an appreciation of working in the diverse Canadian labour force to support workplace integration.
  • (7) Evaluate personal and professional growth through reflective practice to make informed decisions regarding career directions.
