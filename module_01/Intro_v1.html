Introduction

Identifying the right career choice for you involves many factors, but a crucial part of the process is developing your own self-awareness. Taking time to do this at the start of your career planning can significantly impact on your job satisfaction and success.

Module Objectives

  • Recognize how maintaining a good digital brand contributes to achieving success in your career and in this this course.
  • Identify the social media guidelines that should be followed to reflect professionalism.
  • Indicate how applying the principles of reflective practice to personal and professional experiences can enhance your ability to communicate and make informed decisions about your career direction.
  • Recognize the characteristics of strong blogs.
  • • Examine how your past team experiences and team contract can contribute to positive team results in this course.
