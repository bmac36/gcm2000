Legislation and Business Practices

When a Company Violates Employment Standards

Companies face stiff penalties for not complying with employment standards. For example, if an employer is accused of violating terms of Pay Equity, they can face audits, large fines and potential sanctions by the Pay Equity Commission. Violations of Health and Safety obligations could potentially lead to criminal charges. Read about Five Common Legal Issues Faced by Businesses .
