Evaluating Business Structures in Canada

Business Structures

According to Industry Canada and Statistics Canada, there are at least 1,138,761 registered employer businesses in Canada (businesses with at least one employee on payroll)! Many of these businesses operate differently, depending on their size and structure. The way a business is structured is intimately linked with its organizational culture. That’s why understanding the differences between different-sized businesses and different structures is critical for you to understand how to arrive and thrive in the Canadian workplace.

Of the more than 1.1 million companies, 1,107,540 of those were small businesses, comprising 98 per cent of all businesses in Canada.

What exactly is a small business?
