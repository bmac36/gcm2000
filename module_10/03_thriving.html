Thriving at Work

You’ve gone through the interview process and have been offered a job at an organization. How will you know whether you are likely to thrive in that job? Each organization has its own unique personality or corporate culture, which profoundly affects the way work is done and who feels comfortable working there.

You are best positioned for success on the job if you can answer "Yes" to the following questions.

Click each card to learn more.

Is my personality compatible with the personality of the company?

When you consider the true personality of the workplace, is it compatible with your own personality? Consider what you know about yourself from your self-assessments. It will be easier for you to succeed if you share the same priorities, values, beliefs, and attitudes as the group of people with whom you will be working.

Does the company have a strong corporate culture?

Culture is a powerful element that shapes work enjoyment, relationships, and processes. A strong culture is easy to spot—it engages people, creates energy and momentum, and makes everyone more successful. A company with a strong culture is definitely where you want to be working!
