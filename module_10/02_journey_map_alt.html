Part II: Thrive

The second part of the journey will help you thrive on the job. By applying AIDE (Analyze, Identify, Develop, Engage) you will gain the knowledge and experience needed to integrate successfully into the Canadian workforce. This week you will:

* Analyze Canadian workplace structures, leadership models, and other drivers of organizational culture to identify the kind of workplace best suited to you. You will also explore the Canadian legislation that exists to protect you in the workplace.
