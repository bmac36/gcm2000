Leadership and You

Leadership

You may respond better to some styles of leadership better than others. How should you expect to thrive under each different leadership style?

Take the Leadership Assessment Quiz

Understanding a leader’s traits and style can help you understand how to be a better employee and team member, a better follower, and perhaps a better leader too! Take the Leadership Assessment Quiz to test your assumptions about what makes a good leader and to determine your own ability to lead. Bring your results to class.

You may find that your personality is a good fit with leadership and may want to include leadership as a goal or objective in your career SMART goal planning.

Leadership and Gender Expectations

It’s also important to be aware of how gender expectations can influence your view of leadership. Stereotypes, symbols, and social norms can shape your expectations of men and women in leadership. Awareness of these gender differences can lead to greater respect, diversity, and equality in the workplace.
