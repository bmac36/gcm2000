Classroom Activities

Click to reveal an explanation for each activity.

Legislation Review

Time Required: 45 minutes

In small groups, review two of the following key pieces of workplace legislation. Using chart paper, summarize the most important details and present your summaries to the class. Some topics will be covered more than once.

  • Employment Standards Act
  • Occupational Health and Safety Act
  • Pay Equity
  • Bill 168
  • Canadian Human Rights Act
  • Employment Equality
  • PIPEDA
  • AODA

Organizational Structure and Culture Exercise

Time Required: 30 minutes

Students are to walk around room identifying different features of organizations that are structured differently. After they have explored each company, they are to identify which is their preferred destination by walking to the appropriate sheet.

Leadership Exercise

Time Required: 45 minutes

Storytelling exercise on leadership traits and styles. Students identify which leadership traits and styles they find effective and ineffective

Introduction to Group Project Case Study

Time Required: 30 minutes

Instructor will provide more information regarding expectations and completion process for group project case study.

Assignment of Group Project Case Studies

Time Required: 30 minutes

Students will be assigned specific case studies with associated documentation

Beyond the Classroom

Blog Post for Week 10

For your weekly blog, answer ONE of the following questions:

  • Which responsibilities do you have as a worker to understand and comply with workplace regulations and legislation?
  • How are activities such as task allocation, coordination and supervision related to the achievement of organizational aims?
  • What is the relationship between organizational structure and organizational culture?
  • Which types of organizational structure and culture do you prefer?
