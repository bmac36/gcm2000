Why Volunteer?

What are the some of the benefits of volunteering?

Volunteering makes us a better society. Many community organizations could not perform the valuable work that they do without the noble service of the volunteer. But the benefits to volunteering extend beyond maintaining the social fabric of society.

Expand each panel to reveal some of the benefits of volunteering.

Serving Your Community

Volunteering is rich with new experiences and new people. Get out and meet your neighbors from down the street or across the globe. Learn about new cultures and the challenges of the most vulnerable among us. Make new friends while being a part of something larger than you.

Building Your Résumé

Learn more about an organization that you’ve always wanted to work for by spending some time as a volunteer there. Foster significant networks with managers, administrators, clients and other volunteers. Exercise your current skill sets or add some new ones while boosting your career options.
