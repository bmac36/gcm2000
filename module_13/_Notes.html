Research Volunteer Opportunities and Present Findings

Part 1:

In small groups, using this week’s learning material and the Kitchener-Waterloo Volunteer Action Centre website find a volunteer opportunity that offers the opportunity for community engagement, skill development, and chances to expand one’s network.

Part 2:

Create a short slide presentation of five to six slides, highlighting basic information about this volunteer opportunity including name of organization, products or services rendered by the organization, location, size, volunteer opportunities, application process, and how this volunteer work offers personal growth, network, or skill development opportunities.

Part 3:

Present your slide show to the class using your best presentation skills. Be prepared to answer questions at the end of your presentation.

Beyond the Classroom

Blog Post for Week 13

For your weekly blog, reflect upon your learning in the course by answering the following questions:

  1. What are your expectations for "arriving" on your career journey? Have these expectations changed since you started the course? What job search techniques still need to be improved for you to maximize your chances for success?
  2. What do you plan to do to "thrive" in the Canadian workplace?
  3. What will you do to continue your journey of lifelong learning? Do you have plans to develop new skills, refine your abilities, or move towards a new goal?
