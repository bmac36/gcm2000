Uncovering Your Network

5

Summary

Congratulations! You have completed the fifth week of online learning. You should now be able to:

  • Understand the importance of networking for an effective job search.
  • Inventory your network of personal and professional contacts.
  • Identify key thought leaders and professional associations.
  • Conduct informal networking with personal contacts.
  • Identify, research, attend and follow-up on networking events.
  • Arrange, research, conduct and follow-up from informational interviews.
