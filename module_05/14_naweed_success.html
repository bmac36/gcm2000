Uncovering Your Network

5

Naweed’s Success Story

When networking, you will be approaching a variety of people in formal and informal ways, both in-person and online. Across all forms of networking you will must be able to quickly convey what you are looking for and what you have to offer. You also need to make a positive impression to gain credibility.

Review Naweed’s networking success story below. Click the linked sections to learn what approaches helped make this event a success for him.

Starting out, I had no idea where to look or what events were happening. I tried googling "networking events for IT" and searched meetup.com – both came up with great events. Unfortunately, the events were happening during my part-time work schedule so I couldn’t attend. When I checked my twitter feed, I noticed that my college instructor was tweeting about an employer event on campus that was happening on my day off. The employer is a big technology company nearby that comes to the college every term to recruit new grads.

Ahead of time, I carefully practiced my personal introduction and was careful to plan what I would say. I looked up recent tech news to see what was happening with this company, and saw that they just won a national award for innovation.

At the event, I saw the hiring manager and confidently walked over. I offered my hand for a handshake and smiled. I introduced myself and said I’m a new graduate that is very glad to connect. I said I greatly admired his company, and congratulated him for the company’s innovation award, and he smiled even bigger. He said he was impressed that I knew that, and he went into further detail about the work that led to the award. I listened patiently and made some polite inquiries about his role in the project.

After a while the discussion turned back to me. He wanted to know more about my skills and abilities. After I gave him more information about my skills and future goals in a few short sentences, he said he was impressed and that he would like to set up a time to talk in more detail about my future plans. I wrote down his contact information and followed up with him the next day to set up a time to chat at his office.
