Instructions for Week 5 Blog Assignment

Research the networking opportunities available to you in your area. Then select and attend a networking event or meeting to learn more about what opportunities they hold for you. Answer the following questions in your blog:

1.

1. What event did you attend?

2.

2. What occurred when you were there?

3.

3. How did the experience challenge you? In what ways did it benefit you?

4.

4. How do you intend to use networking events to develop employment opportunities in the future?
