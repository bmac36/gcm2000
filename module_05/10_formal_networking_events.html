You may come across such events as:

  • Employer info sessions
  • Career fairs
  • Conferences
  • Meetups
  • Networking breakfasts
  • Expositions, trade shows, and industry shows
  • Social events
  • Workshops or lectures

Networking events are a great place to start developing contacts and growing your network. Some events will have the explicit purpose of connecting job seekers with employers, while others will be industry-specific and focus on the transfer of information.

Regardless of the explicit purpose, you need to identify which events are occurring and find out how to make meaningful impressions with the right contacts. Your ultimate goal with networking events should be to meet new people, develop a positive reputation, and make an impression that will motivate others to help you uncover opportunities.
