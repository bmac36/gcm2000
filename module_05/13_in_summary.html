In Summary: TEDed Video

You have now considered the informal and formal networking opportunities available to you and how these can lead to job opportunities. To summarize what you’ve learned, watch the TedEd video Networking for the networking averse. It demonstrates how leads typically come from “weak leaks”—acquaintances or people we see infrequently, rather than close friends or family members. It shows how the energy you put into networking can pay off in the long run.
