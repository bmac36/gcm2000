Benefits of Employer Research

Most people search for suitable occupations without taking the time to learn about employers, key contacts, and organizations. Spending time conducting systematic research about employers will help you in many ways!

How Does It Help?

Employer research helps you to:

  • Evaluate if the employer is a good fit for you
  • Uncover the employer’s needs so you can create a custom ‘pitch’
  • Learn about recent happenings, products, updates
  • Become knowledgeable regarding the company history, who works there, what their mission or values are.
  • Identify ways to connect with the employer or individuals via networking, informational interviews, and on social media
  • Create customized resumes and cover letters and prepare for effective job interviews (including the questions
