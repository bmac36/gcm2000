Video Summary

If you think about it, you probably have a number of preferred sources for information. Typing some keywords in Google, looking up weather or news using Twitter or a mobile app, or reading a newspaper are just some of the common ways of finding information. When doing academic research, it’s important to seek out credible and varied sources. As a Conestoga student you have direct access to over a hundred research database materials for free, including e-books, journal articles, newspaper articles, and book reviews. This is just the tip of the iceberg in terms of what’s available.
