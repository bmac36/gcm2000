What is Plagiarism?

A few of my friends directly copied online articles for a recent assignment. They got caught and not only ended up failing the course but also getting kicked out of school. I learned quickly about the importance of avoiding plagiarism by citing all of my information sources.

How do I reference?

I always document the sources I use to support my ideas in my essays, presentations, blogs, or reports. Why? It protects me from plagiarism for one, but also gives me credibility as a writer, and lets readers easily cross-reference my sources. Staying accountable to my original source material is very important.
