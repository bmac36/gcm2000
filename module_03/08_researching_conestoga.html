How do I get better search results?

I used to just do a Google search on ’Data Analyst‘ and other job titles of interest but I didn’t get a lot of helpful results. Defining more specific keywords at the Library helped me to find better information about career options.

What resourcess can I access?

Sure, Google gives me fast results but the Discover Service at the Library is just as fast and gives me free access to a wide range of credible resources like books, ebooks, Journal articles and newspapers for free.
