Group Project Assignment

Part 3: Final Presentation and Performance Review

Groups will deliver a 30-minute presentation next week about their solution to the client in front of the other class groups. Students will present what their case was, what they proposed as a solution, what challenges they encountered, and what the final product was. Each group member will be responsible to deliver part of the presentation. The use of visual aids (e.g., PowerPoint) is strongly recommended. Following the presentation, students will give feedback to fellow group members using the Group Feedback Form .
