Group Project Presentations

14

Introduction

There is no online learning component this week. Your focus will be delivering an in-class presentation about your group project in front of the other class groups. You will give feedback to the other members of your group and record the feedback you receive in the Group Feedback Worksheet. You can also begin completing the Self-Reflection and Personal Feedback Form, which will guide you in conducting a performance review of the quality of your project, your individual performance, and the performance of each group member during the group project.
