Group Project Case Study: Part 3 Final Presentation

Time Required: Entire Duration of Class

Groups will deliver a 30-minute presentation this week about their solution to the client in front of the other class groups. Students will present on what their case was, what they proposed as a solution, what challenges they encountered, and what the final product was. Each group member will be responsible to deliver part of the presentation. The use of visual aids (e.g., PowerPoint) is strongly recommended.

Group Feedback Form

Following the presentation:

  1. Each group member will give feedback to the other members of their team, as documented in Part A of their Group Feedback Form.
  2. Students will record the feedback they receive from each team member in Part B of their Group Feedback Form.
  3. Once the Group Feedback Form is complete, it should be handed in to the Instructor

Self-Reflection and Personal Feedback Form

Following their presentation, students can begin completing the Self-Reflection and Personal Feedback Form, which will guide them in conducting a performance review of the quality of their solution, their individual performance, and the performance of each group member during the group project.
