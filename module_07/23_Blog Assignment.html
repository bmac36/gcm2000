Instructions for Week 7 Blog Assignment

For your blog this week:

Imagine that you have created a strong set of job search tools that have landed you an interview in a job in your field. However, before going to the interview, the recruiter has asked that you write a statement for them that walks them through your résumé and emphasizes your transferable skills.

1.

1. Write a statement that responds to the recruiter’s request. How would you explain your résumé in a statement?

2.

2. How would you use your résumé to focus on transferable skills?

Hint:

Focus on academic, professional, and extra-curricular experience that demonstrates what you have learned, and refer to your transferable skills from your Self-Assessment Summary.
