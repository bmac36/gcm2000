Intern – Mobile Development Job

Location: Montreal, QC, CA

Company: SAP

Company Description

As market leader in enterprise application software, SAP helps companies of all sizes and industries innovate through simplification. From the back office to the boardroom, warehouse to storefront, on premise to cloud, desktop to mobile device – SAP empowers people and organizations to work together more efficiently and use business insight more effectively to stay ahead of the competition. SAP applications and services enable customers to operate profitably, adapt continuously, and grow sustainably.

Job Duties

The Mission of P&I Industry Cloud is to accelerate customer value and growth in strategic, core and incubation industries, the LOB’s Manufacturing and Supply Chain and the rising topic of Internet of Things (IoT) / Industry 4.0. As P&I Industry Cloud – Mobility team we are right in the middle of all the latest trends. When everything is connected – people, devices, businesses – entire business models can be radically transformed. But instead of transforming around a concept, we can transform around individuals. In the end, mobility is contemporaneous; it’s real time. The great promise of simplicity is for businesses to be context-aware so they can create engaging, seamless experiences for every individual – wherever they are. This includes establishing and delivering strategies and mobile solutions for on-premise, public and private cloud in close collaboration with other teams in development (LoB and Technology) and the field. Leveraging user centric design (FIORI paradigm), design and design thinking principles and SAP HANA and creating new and extending existing working models.

We are looking for a highly-motivated mobile developer to join our Industry Mobility Team to build compelling native and hybrid mobile business applications for iOS, Android and Windows platforms, in close collaboration with customers and end-users. This challenging opportunity is perfect for developers with excellent mobile programming skills, an open mind and willingness to join a “start-up” like team.

Team

Service Industries team: SAP is the world's largest supplier of enterprise management software and employs about 450 persons in SAP Labs Canada in Montreal, and 800 in Vancouver. The Service Industries-Utilities team is part of Application Innovation CRM SD & SI Apps Unit and is responsible for developing concepts and new business applications for the Utilities industry, e.g. Multichannel Foundation for Utilities.

Job Duties

The Intern will be part of a Scrum team whose focus is to deliver a customer engagement mobile and Augmented Reality solutions for different service industries. Intern is expected work on a variety of interesting topics including cloud and mobility and web. Responsibilities may include,

  • Develop mobile applications on different device platforms (incl. Wearables) like iPad/iPhone/iWatch, Android, Windows 8 using the entire SAP portfolio for mobile computing.
  • Develop mission critical mobile applications connecting users in the field with enterprise applications, SAP and non-SAP back-ends.
  • Design and development of new features and improvement of existing ones.
  • Testing and Bug fixing
  • Participation in design and code reviews
  • Documentation
  • Research on topics assigned to the team and prototype if needed.
  • Work closely with product architects and customers to holistically understand business and user requirements, and continuously improve own products to really provide software which users love to work with.

Skills & Abilities

  • Undergrad (2nd or 3rd year) or Master student, or new graduate
  • Software engineering, Computer engineering or Computing Science
  • Hands-on experience design and development experience with an object oriented language preferably Java.
  • Experience with web technologies such as HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, and JQuery, Node.js.
  • Experience with mobile technologies and platforms like Android, iOs and Windows.
  • Good practical knowledge of REST Web services, OData and the new Web technologies (Web2.0)
  • Very good English communications skills, verbal and written
  • Desirable: Experience with source code versioning tools such as Github.
  • Desirable: knowledge of ABAP and of SAP software
  • Desirable: knowledge of enterprise management software
