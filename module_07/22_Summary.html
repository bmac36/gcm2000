Job Search Tools

7

Summary

Congratulations! You have completed the seventh week of online learning.

Having professional job search tools requires time, effort, and patience. It takes practice to learn how to seek out the right job postings, properly interpret the postings, and analyze your inventory of skills against job requirements. Crafting the appropriate tools to respond to each posting also takes practice. Taking the time to find the right posting and produce thoughtful and customized documents will help you achieve a more efficient rate of response. When used in tandem with effective networking, informational interviews, social media engagement, and building upon LMI and industry trends research, you will have dramatically increased your potential for success by having a holistic job search strategy. More on that next week!

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

  • Understand the purpose of developing strong job search tools.
  • Analyze job postings and compare personal inventory of skills, abilities, and qualifications against job requirements.
  • Evaluate different job search tool structures and their applicability for specific job postings.
  • Prepare customized, targeted, and industry/employer-specific resume, cover letter, and thank-you note.
