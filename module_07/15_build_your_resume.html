Build Your Resume

Now it is time for you to write a résumé for the job posting provided this week. Make sure that you can check off all of the items in the list below before coming to class. Your goal is to get an interview, and you don’t want your résumé to make a poor first impression.

Content

  • Write to the job posting
  • Focus on your accomplishments, not your job duties
  • Ensure your email is professional (use your Conestoga College account)
  • When you use dates, make sure you use reverse chronological (present to past) within sections
  • No personal pronouns (I, my)
  • Do not include photos or personal information such as marital status, political affiliation or religious affiliation
  • Do not include references within your résumé content, unless asked to do so

Format

  • No grammar or spelling errors – this is very important!
  • Use the correct tense
  • Consistent font (use the same throughout) and spacing
  • Consistent layout
  • Use point form, always!
  • Keep your résumé to two pages or fewer
  • Your name, email and phone number at the top of both pages
