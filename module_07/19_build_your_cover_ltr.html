Build Your Cover Letter

You will also write a cover letter for the job posting provided this week. There is no such thing as a generic cover letter and employers can spot the difference.

Make sure that you can check off all of the items in the list below before bringing your cover letter to class.

  • Personal header that matches résumé
  • Employer’s address (on left hand side of the letter)
  • Date of application
  • Job title and competition number (find this on the job posting)
  • Name of hiring manager (call to find out)
  • Paragraph structure: Introduction, two to three middle paragraphs, conclusion
  • Sincerely
  • Signature
  • Enclosure
  • No grammar or spelling errors – this is very important!
  • Keep your cover letter to one page
