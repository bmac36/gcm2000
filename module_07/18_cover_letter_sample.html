Ms. Linda Fitzgerald

St. Mary’s General Hospital
911 Queen’s Blvd.
Kitchener, ON N3J 4X3

Re: Health Records Technician position, Search #16-003

Dear Ms. Fitzgerald:

As a recent graduate of the Health Office Administration program at Conestoga College, and a current St. Mary’s volunteer, I am interested in the position of Health Records Technician, as advertised on the St. Mary’s General Hospital Careers web page on January 7, 2016. I am passionate about the holistic approach to healthcare offered at St. Mary’s.

The combination of my education and training, as well as related employment experience matches the position requirements that you seek. I have practical experience working within a medical office setting as demonstrated through my practicum position at St. Mary’s Hospital as an Assistant Office Administrator in the Health Records department. I bring the ability to type 60 words per minute, experience with the Meditech System as well as 3M, and proven medical terminology and transcription skills.

In addition, I bring exceptional communication skills, and a cheerful and professional interpersonal matter. As you will note from my résumé, I have extensive experience in customer service, and an excellent work ethic and reliability record. I would be happy to provide you with further documentation in the way of references and achievement letters from past employers.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I would welcome the opportunity to speak with you in person to further discuss my related qualification and enthusiasm for the position of Health Records Technician at St. Mary’s Hospital.

Sincerely,

 

Emily Dane [signature]
