Reverse Chronological Résumé: Sample

A reverse chronological résumé includes the employment history from the most recent employment and works backward in time. This style emphasizes a steady work experience with clear growth and progress. Employers often prefer the reverse chronological résumé because it’s clear, concise, and easy to read.

Examine this sample of a chronological résumé. Click on the information sign for tips on writing that section of your résumé.
