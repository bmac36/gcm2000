Show your Fit to the Job

Starting with the posting is a smart way of developing a customized application. The previous topic deconstructed the job posting and explained what you should look for. Now is the time to show how your experience and education contributes to the skills required of the job.

1.

1. List each requirement for the job. If there are too many requirements to address, identify the most important requirements. Include a variety of required qualifications, technical skills and transferable skills. Review the previous topic if you need a reminder of what to look for.

2.

2. Add your specific experience that meets or exceeds that requirement. This may come from your education, your work experience or even from volunteering. When you are starting out, pull in your experience from your full life.

3.

3. Describe an accomplishment that demonstrates how you meet the requirement. We will learn more in Writing Accomplishment Statements about how to write a solid accomplishment statement.

Use the job posting in the next topic to apply this process.
