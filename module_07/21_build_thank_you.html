Build a Thank You Note

The final document you will prepare this week is the thank you note. Although three options for sending a thank you note were discussed, for this class you will write a word-processed letter. Bring a draft of your thank you to class for feedback.

Use a formal business letter format for your thank you note. The following template is a good way to organize your letter. Also review this example follow-up letter sent through email .

Paragraph 1:

This two- or three-sentence paragraph explains why you are writing.

Paragraph 2:

This paragraph reminds the interviewer why your specific skills are a match for the job and how you will add value to their organization. This might also be an opportunity to address any of your areas of weakness during the interview or to improve upon an answer you gave during the interview.

Paragraph 3:

In this paragraph, emphasize your gratitude for the opportunity and state your interest in being the successful candidate.
