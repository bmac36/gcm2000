Once you have completed the test, you will be able to see whether the test indicates that you have an automatic biases regarding people with disabilities.

Reflective Questions

  1. Were you surprised by the result?
  2. What can you do to better recognize, understand, engage, and celebrate diversity related to people with disabilities?
  3. How can you benefit from working on a team with members whose ability may be different from your own?
Consider taking another IAT to assess your automatic biases in other areas and reflect upon how you can do better to eliminate these biases.
