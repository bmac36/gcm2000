Meet Your Mentors

Throughout the online portion of this course, you will be supported by “past graduates” who have successfully secured employment. Acting as your online mentors, these fictional characters will provide you with tips, tricks, and proven strategies to help you to succeed in your own career journey.

Naweed

Naweed is a people person with a strong commitment to customer service. He is observant and practical, and always eager to share his insights.

Jenn

Jenn is skeptical of new ideas and learns best by doing. She is a good athlete who has always enjoyed teamwork and helping others realize their potential.

Anwar

Anwar is quiet and a bit of a history buff. He’s continually assessing the people and circumstances in his environment to better understand the world.

Charizar

Charizar is outgoing and creative with a zest for life. She is an entrepreneur who is committed to succeeding in the larger corporate world.
