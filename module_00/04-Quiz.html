Evaluations

Quizzes

These assessments are designed to test students’ comprehension of the key concepts, terms, and themes of Weeks 1 – 9.

Weeks 1 – 9 Quizzes

Each quiz is made up of 10 randomly selected questions, each worth 1 mark.

These online quizzes will be delivered in-class by the instructor. Each quiz is worth 10% of the 10% total weight for all quizzes, with the exception of the Week 1 quiz which is worth 20% of the 10% as it covers both the Orientation and Week 1 content.

Value: 10% final grade.
