Discussion Topics

The following sections contain the questions to guide your weekly group discussions in Career Management in Canada. Go to Guidelines for Online Discussion Boards to read more about the expectations and directions for using discussion boards on eConestoga. The discussion boards give students the opportunity to demonstrate that they have read and critically reflected on the material for discussion. It also allows students to challenge themselves and others to investigate and evaluate the material.

Week 1 – Your Career Journey Begins

Initial post due: By Friday (11:55 p.m.) of Week 1

All responses due: Before the next class

Please answer the following questions for your initial post:

  1. In what area did you score highest for your eLearnReady assessment? What area did you score the lowest? Do you agree with these results? Why or why not?
  2. Based on your assessment results report, what are two study tips that eLearnReady suggests to help you imporve your lowest area of study readiness? How can you implement these study habits into your daily life?
  3. What was your score for Course Management System? Do any areas of the course management system confuse or frustrate you?

Please post a reply to each team member’s post.

You are encouraged to contribute to the ongoing conversation from your reply or other member’s replies. Ensure that you post a reply to each team member at a minimum, and adhere to the ground rules for creating a safe and supportive academic environment.

Note: Always compile and save your work in a word processing program first and then copy and paste your work accordingly.

Week 2 – Self-Assessment

Initial post due: By Friday (11:55 p.m.) of Week 2

All responses due: Before the next class

Please answer the following questions for your initial post:

  1. What is the purpose of the self-assessment process in career planning? Summarize what assessments you completed and provide a brief overview of what you learned.
  2. Describe your interests, as discovered in the self-awareness assessments you have completed. What did you discover to be your strongest interests? How have you demonstrated each one?
  3. Describe your Technical, Industry Specific, and Transferable Skills (see Ontario Skills Passport). Which did you discover to be your strongest technical, industry, and/or transferable skills?

Week 7 – Job Search Tools

Initial post due: By Friday (11:55 p.m.) of week 7

All responses due: Before the next class

Please answer the following questions for your initial post.

  1. What job posting websites have you used? Which do you find the best site? Which sites do you not like or find difficult to navigate?
