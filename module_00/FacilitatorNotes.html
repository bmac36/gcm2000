How to Edit the "Meet Your Instructor" Page

It will be your responsibility to edit the "Meet Your Instructor" page. You will find the steps to follow below. If at any point you have any questions or concerns, you can contact eConestoga with a Maintenance Request.

Step One: Your Photo

Placeholder for your Instructor photograph

Example of replaced instructor photo

It is your responsibility to upload your own personal photograph to replace the placeholder.

Find an image of yourself that is appropriate. Alter its size to the dimensions shown in the placeholder (300x250). Most image editing software will allow you to do this. Resize and / or crop your photo to meet this size.

When you have your image prepared, be sure to name it exactly like this; InstructorPhoto.jpg. If you do not use this name, the picture will not appear.

Upload the image to the course via Edit Course > Manage Files. Proceed with the upload, and overwrite any files that have the same filename.

Return to the "Meet Your Instructor" page to make sure your photo has replaced the previous graphic.

Editing Your Welcome Message

Beside the instructor photo, you'll see some text that looks like this.

Use this area to write a quick welcome message to your class, maybe a little something about yourself and how you're looking forward to teaching this class, or how your students can feel free to contact you.

In order to edit this text, use the button underneath the page's content. This button to allows you to edit the text. When when done, select the button at the bottom of the text window.

Editing Your Bio

Exactly like editing your introductory text, use the button underneath the page's content. This button allows you to edit the text. When done, select the button at the bottom of the text window.
