Bio

Acknowledged as an authority in career management in the post-secondary sector, I have been providing career management services to students since 2010, with a clientele spanning multiple fields, departments, and career stages. Testimonies from my clients and students have been overwhelming, and I am honoured to have been considered among the top instructors at York University in 2012. My work with graduate students’ career development has led me to develop a professional interest in refining labour market research methods, rethinking career management strategies, and perfecting job search tools.

My past includes a distinguished academic career, specifically pursuing a Ph.D. and teaching in the Faculty of Arts at York University, where I specialized in law, government, and history. It is my background in research that has enabled me to assist my clients to discover their goals, skills, and abilities to produce strong job search strategies. I have developed a breadth and depth of knowledge as a human relations professional, where I spent time in recruiting, organizational development, and compliance roles.

As your instructor, I am excited to welcome you to the course, and to remind you that we’re embarking on this learning experience together. The tools, techniques, and systems you learn in this course will be directly applicable to your job search and career development, so I encourage you to make the most of our time together.
